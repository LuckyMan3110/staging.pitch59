What is Virtual Video Shoot?

By helping you shoot your pitch video (usually in just 20 or 30 minutes), and then editing it for you, Pitch59’s Virtual Video Shoots make creating your video a breeze.

You’ll need:

  • - A camera to shoot the video (usually your phone),
  • - A computer to video conference with your PitchCoach, and
  • - To determine the lighting and background that you want in your video.

Don’t worry about memorizing or preparing a script - you won’t need it! Your PitchCoach will interview you and guide you through the process.
